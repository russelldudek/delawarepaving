Automation Load Test

Use this one-page review before committing a workflow to production. Score evidence, not enthusiasm.

Field truthHave we observed the real workflow, exceptions, artifacts, timing, and user burden?
Outcome ownerIs one business owner accountable for the result and one service owner accountable for operation?
Decision rightsAre approvals, overrides, exception authority, and escalation paths explicit?
Data continuityAre sources, required fields, evidence, quality rules, retention, and traceability defined?
System jointIs the interface between ERP, Microsoft 365, and field tools governed, testable, and recoverable?
Human authorityIs it clear what AI or automation may do and what remains a human decision?
Economic baselineDo we know current cycle time, handling, rework, delay, exception cost, or another meaningful baseline?
Adoption pathDoes the mechanism remove enough burden to displace the workaround, with training and feedback built in?
Production supportAre monitoring, errors, retries, access, runbook, change control, recovery, and retirement covered?
BUILD / SCALEEvidence is strong across layers; proceed with bounded production scope and instrumentation.
STABILIZE FIRSTValue exists, but ownership, data, process, control, or support cannot yet carry production load.
OBSERVE MOREThe problem, baseline, user burden, or decision is not understood well enough to automate responsibly.
